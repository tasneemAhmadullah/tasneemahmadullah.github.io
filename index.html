Dr. Tasneem Ahmadullah

Postdoctoral Researcher - Pacific Northwest National Laboratory

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL) focused on unraveling the complexities of environmental soil systems. My research combines advanced analytical techniques with comprehensive modeling approaches to gain deeper insights into soil-environmental interactions and their broader ecological implications.

My expertise includes spectroscopic and spectrometric analysis of soil samples and biogeochemical and geochemical modeling of environmental systems. I received my PhD in Environmental Engineering from the University of Connecticut.

I am passionate about environmental science and always interested in learning new spectroscopic techniques and modeling approaches. My ultimate research goal is to contribute meaningful solutions to environmental preservation and build pathways toward a more sustainable world.
